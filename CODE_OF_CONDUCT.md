# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Vertical Design MD a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected behavior

Demonstrate empathy and kindness, respect differing viewpoints, give and accept constructive feedback, take responsibility for mistakes, and focus on what is best for the community. Domain disagreements should be resolved through evidence and explicit uncertainty rather than authority or personal attacks.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, sexualized language, trolling, insults, threats, doxxing, publishing private information without permission, and other conduct inappropriate in a professional setting are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through their GitHub profiles. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates this code. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.

This code is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html), version 2.1.
