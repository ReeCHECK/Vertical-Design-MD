# Healthcare Workstation Safety and Compliance

## Mandatory product rules

- Preserve patient, encounter, author, source, observation time, and version context for clinical information.
- Apply minimum-necessary data exposure and trusted authorization to reads as well as writes.
- Record security- and clinical-significant access, changes, signatures, overrides, exports, and corrections in appropriate audit systems.
- Keep draft, unsigned, entered-in-error, corrected, and signed information visually and semantically distinct.
- Require explicit governed behavior for identity conflict, emergency access, and high-consequence medication actions.
- Exclude unnecessary sensitive data from telemetry, crash reports, notifications, and copied links.

## Medication safety

Medication presentation keeps name, strength, dose, route, frequency, units, source, and status together. Similar names must not be differentiated only through capitalization or color. Decision support is attributable, current, dismissible only according to policy, and never presented as unquestionable truth.

## Privacy and audit

Screens use privacy-aware defaults for shared environments, session timeout, printing, export, and shoulder-surfing risk. The product defines who may access audit records and how corrections are represented without destroying history.

## Review boundary

This pack is not a clinical protocol, medical-device submission, privacy compliance program, or substitute for local law. Qualified clinical safety, privacy, security, legal, accessibility, and human-factors review is required.
